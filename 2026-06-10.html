Lead Story

Chase is paying 55% to move points into Marriott — the richest Bonvoy bonus of the year

Through June 30, Chase Ultimate Rewards is running a 55% transfer bonus to Marriott Bonvoy, meaning 10,000 UR points land as 15,500 Bonvoy points. That is the most generous Chase-to-Marriott promo we have seen in 2026, and it lines up neatly with summer-travel award pricing. Marriott points are usually a soft transfer target — the 1:1 baseline rarely justifies moving flexible currency — but at 1:1.55 the math flips for anyone eyeing a Category 6–7 property or a five-night-fourth-free redemption. If you have a confirmed stay where the Bonvoy price beats cash, this is the window. Chase also opened a separate 30% bonus to Virgin Atlantic Flying Club (June 9–July 14), a far more interesting target for award flights via ANA, Delta and Air France-KLM.
