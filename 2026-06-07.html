Lead Story

Chase is paying 55% extra on Marriott transfers — the richest hotel transfer bonus of the year. Through June 30, Chase Ultimate Rewards points move to Marriott Bonvoy at 1:1.55 instead of the usual 1:1. Transfers to Marriott are normally a poor value (Bonvoy points are worth roughly half a UR point), which is exactly why a 55% premium matters: it narrows the gap enough to make sense for a specific, priced-out redemption — think a five-night award where Marriott's "fifth night free" stacks on top.

The math: 60,000 UR becomes 93,000 Bonvoy points. Pair that with tonight's Escapes booking deadline above and a peak-summer Marriott stay gets meaningfully cheaper either way — cash at 20% off, or points at an effective 55% discount on transfers. As always, don't transfer speculatively; UR points are far more flexible than Bonvoy points. The Points Guy has the full rundown.
