Lead Story

The June 30 cliff: Marriott transfer bonuses stack up across Chase and Amex

If you have a Marriott redemption on the horizon, the next twelve days are the window. Chase Ultimate Rewards is running a 55% transfer bonus to Marriott Bonvoy — the richest Chase-to-Marriott ratio in recent memory — meaning 1,000 Chase points become 1,550 Bonvoy points. It expires June 30.

Amex is leaning the same direction. Membership Rewards is offering a 20% bonus to Marriott Bonvoy and a 25% bonus to Air France-KLM Flying Blue, both also closing June 30. After a notably quiet stretch — Amex transfer bonuses have all but vanished in 2026 — this is the closest thing to a flurry we've seen this year, and history suggests the next one could be a long way off.

The caution, as always: only transfer when you have a specific award in mind. Points moved into a hotel or airline program can't come back, and Marriott's dynamic award pricing means a 55% bonus is only a deal if the night you want hasn't been inflated to absorb it. Confirm availability first, transfer second. Details at Frequent Miler and The Points Guy.

Today's Briefs

Hyatt's Chase ratio quietly worsens for new cardholders

Chase confirmed that the Sapphire Preferred and Ink Business Preferred now transfer to World of Hyatt at 4:3 instead of 1:1 for anyone who applied on or after June 15. Existing cardholders keep the 1:1 rate until September 30, then move to 4:3 on October 1. Hyatt has long been the crown jewel of Chase's transfer partners; if you hold an older card, your 1:1 window is finite. More.

United makes pooled miles vastly more useful

United lifted the biggest restriction on MileagePlus miles pooling: pooled miles can now book awards on 40+ partner airlines — Air Canada, Lufthansa, ANA and more — not just United-operated flights. For families and travel partners banking miles together, this turns pooling from a domestic-only convenience into a real tool for long-haul international premium cabins. More.

Delta's Amex cards add a second free checked bag

Effective June 4, the SkyMiles American Express lineup now grants a complimentary second checked bag on U.S. domestic Delta-operated flights, on top of the existing first-bag benefit. Amex and Delta also refreshed the entire card design, including a new Slate Grey colorway on the Reserve cards. A modest but real boost for families flying Delta. More.

Hyatt's summer stay bonus is worth registering for

Through July 31, World of Hyatt members earn 3,000 bonus points per qualifying stay of 3+ nights at Hyatt Place and Hyatt Select properties in the Americas — up to 30,000 bonus points across the promotion. Registration is required, so opt in before your next booking. More.
