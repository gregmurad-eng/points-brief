Lead StoryChase Dangles a 55% Marriott Bonus — the Richest Hotel Transfer of the Year

Through June 30, Chase is running a 55% transfer bonus from Ultimate Rewards to Marriott Bonvoy, pushing the ratio to a remarkable 1:1.55. Move 100,000 Chase points and 155,000 Bonvoy points land in your account — enough to reshape a summer hotel budget in a single transfer.

The math only works if you have a redemption ready. Marriott points hover around 0.7–0.8 cents each, so a 55% bump roughly offsets Chase's typical 1:1 value premium and then some — but only against a confirmed, high-rate award night. Bonvoy's no-blackout standard rooms and fifth-night-free on points stays are where this shines. Stockpiling without a booking in mind is the classic trap, since transfers are irreversible.

Worth noting: Amex is simultaneously offering a smaller 20% bonus to Marriott, so if your points live on the Amex side, you still have a path — just a less generous one. Read the breakdown at Frequent Miler and The Points Guy.
