Lead Story

Chase's 55% Marriott bonus is the loudest deal on the board — and it dies June 30

For five more days, Chase Ultimate Rewards points move to Marriott Bonvoy at a 1 : 1.55 ratio — a 55% bonus that ranks among the richest Chase has ever run to a hotel partner. Park 60,000 UR and 93,000 Bonvoy points land in your account. (The Points Guy)

The usual caution applies: only transfer against a stay you can actually price out, since Bonvoy points are worth roughly 0.7 cents and UR are worth far more flexibly. But if you've been eyeing a category 6–8 property where the cash rate is brutal, this bonus is the cleanest way Greg's Chase balance turns into a real hotel night. The matching Amex → Bonvoy 20% bonus also closes June 30 — weaker, but live if your points sit on the Amex side. Decide before the month flips.

Hotels · World of Hyatt

Hyatt's five-tier chart is now reality — and points are worth less

The new award chart that took effect May 20 has settled in, moving Hyatt from three tiers to five and reshuffling 136 properties (100+ moved up, 24 down). TPG cut its valuation from 1.65 to 1.55 cents per point. Hyatt is still the best-value hotel currency in Greg's stack, but the easy outsized sweet spots are thinner now — reprice any saved redemptions. (NerdWallet)

Promo · Hyatt

20% points back on Las Vegas award stays

Register, then book and complete an award stay at a participating Vegas Hyatt by July 31 to get 20% of your redeemed World of Hyatt points refunded. Effectively a discount on award nights in a market where cash rates spike with resort fees. (The Points Guy)
