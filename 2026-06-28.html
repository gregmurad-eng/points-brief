Lead Story — The June 30 Cliff

This is the most consequential 48 hours on the points calendar this quarter. Three high-value transfer bonuses and one transfer partnership all expire when June closes, and they happen to sit on top of two of the programs in your stack.

The headline is Chase's 55% bonus to Marriott Bonvoy — among the richest Chase has ever run to a hotel partner. At that rate, 10,000 Ultimate Rewards become 15,500 Bonvoy points. It's compelling math only if you have a specific Marriott redemption lined up; Bonvoy points are worth materially less per point than Ultimate Rewards in the abstract, so this is a "transfer to book," not a "transfer to bank" move. The same caution applies to Amex's 20% to Marriott.

More structurally important: after 3:00 AM ET on June 30, Etihad Guest leaves Amex's transfer chart entirely. If you've been parking value there or planning a First Class redemption, this is your last window to move points across — and even then, only with a confirmed booking in hand, since transfers are irreversible.
