Lead Story — Chase Opens the Vault

Chase is running one of the richest transfer windows of the year, and the headline is a 55% bonus to Marriott Bonvoy running through June 30. That ratio turns 1,000 Ultimate Rewards points into 1,550 Bonvoy points — the strongest hotel transfer bonus active across any program right now. For a category 6 or 7 award, that swing can cover most of a free night on its own.

The catch worth your attention is timing. Chase is simultaneously offering a 30% bonus to Southwest Rapid Rewards, but that one closes tomorrow, June 5. Southwest awards price dynamically off cash fares, so the math is cleanest on pricier last-minute routes — and with the Companion Pass economics, transferred points can effectively fly two people. Marriott can wait three weeks; Southwest cannot wait a day.

Meanwhile Amex remains conspicuously quiet. After a near-total drought of transfer bonuses in 2026, the two it does have on the board — 25% to Flying Blue and a modest 20% to Marriott — look thin next to Chase's offers. If your points are flexible across both currencies, Chase is where the leverage is this week.
