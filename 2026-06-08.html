Lead Story

Chase finally blinks: a rare 55% bonus to Marriott Bonvoy

Chase Ultimate Rewards almost never sweetens its transfer partners, which is what makes this one worth a second look. Through June 30, Chase points convert to Marriott Bonvoy at a 55% bonus — 1,000 points become 1,550. The honest caveat: Marriott is among Chase's weakest partners on a cents-per-point basis, so this only shines if you already have a specific Bonvoy redemption in mind, ideally a high-category property or a points-and-cash stay where Marriott's value holds up. If you don't have a target, leave the points in Chase, where they're worth far more through United, Hyatt, or the travel portal. Pair this with the simultaneous Amex 20% bonus to Marriott and you have two routes to top up the same account before month-end.
