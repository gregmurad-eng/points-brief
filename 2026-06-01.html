Lead Story

June Opens With Fresh Bonuses — Amex Is Back, Citi Adds Qatar

May's transfer bonus drought is over. June brings Amex back into the game with Marriott and Avios, plus a strong new Citi→Qatar play.

After a quiet May, June opens with a reset of the transfer bonus landscape. Two notable additions: Amex→Marriott Bonvoy 20% through June 30 (1:1.2 rate), and Citi→Qatar Avios 30% through June 30 (1:1.3). Both are meaningful.

The Amex→Marriott bonus is particularly interesting alongside Chase's 55% bonus to the same program. If you hold both UR and MR, you now have two elevated paths into Marriott Bonvoy — useful for stacking points ahead of a summer hotel stay. UR at 1.55x is still the better deal, but having MR at 1.2x as a second source is a genuine option.

The Citi→Qatar 30% bonus opens up Oneworld at a discount. Qatar Avios are one of the best currencies for business class redemptions — Qsuite is consistently rated the world's best business product, and Oneworld partners include American, British Airways, Cathay Pacific, and JAL. 30,000 Citi ThankYou points becomes 39,000 Qatar Avios. That's enough for a one-way domestic in business on American, or a building block toward a long-haul Qsuite redemption.

The Chase→Marriott 55% remains the headline bonus — five more weeks to move UR at the best hotel transfer rate available anywhere right now.
