The June 30 reckoning — lock your transfers before the wave breaks

The back half of June is shaping up as a coordinated exit. For a stack as broad as Greg’s, that’s four of the eight programs touched in a single afternoon.

If a summer Promo Reward is on the radar, this is the week to pre-position miles — transfers are irreversible, so only move what a confirmed award needs.
