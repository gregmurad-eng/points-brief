Flying Blue Bonus Closes Tonight — The Case For Moving Now

After today, the Chase→Flying Blue 20% bonus is gone. Here's the honest assessment of whether it's worth your transfer.

The Chase→Flying Blue 20% transfer bonus ends tonight at 11:59pm Eastern. Every 100,000 Ultimate Rewards you move today becomes 120,000 Flying Blue miles. After midnight, you're back to 1:1 — and there's no replacement bonus on the calendar.

The honest case for moving now: Flying Blue promo awards are genuinely good. Monthly discounted business class to Europe runs 40,000–55,000 miles one-way on Air France and KLM. With 120K miles from 100K UR, you're covering a round-trip in business with miles to spare. Flying Blue miles don't expire with account activity.

The honest case against: if you have no Europe trip in the next 24 months and no specific redemption in mind, speculative transfers carry risk — devaluations happen. Flying Blue has been relatively stable, but nothing is guaranteed.

Bottom line: if Europe is in your plans within two years and you have 80K+ UR, today's transfer is a good decision. If you're purely speculating, it's a reasonable bet but not a lock.
