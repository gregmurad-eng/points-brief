Lead Story

The 55% window closes tomorrow

For two weeks Chase Ultimate Rewards has been pushing a 55% transfer bonus to Marriott Bonvoy — among the richest Chase-to-Marriott bonuses on record. At that rate, 10,000 Chase points become 15,500 Bonvoy. It ends at 11:59 PM ET on June 30.

The catch is the same as always with hotel transfers: Marriott points are worth less per point than transferable currency, so this only makes sense if you have a specific, confirmed booking in mind — a Category 6–8 redemption, a Ritz-Carlton stay, or topping off an account for a fixed-rate award. Speculative transfers, even at 55%, usually lock value into the weakest currency in your stack. Pair it with the fact that Amex is simultaneously running 25% to Flying Blue and 20% to Bonvoy expiring the same night, and tomorrow is the real deadline of the month.
