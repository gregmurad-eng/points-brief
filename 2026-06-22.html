Lead Story

Chase's 55% Bonus to Marriott Is the Headline Transfer of the Month

The richest standing transfer bonus on the board right now belongs to Chase: move Ultimate Rewards to Marriott Bonvoy and you'll land 1,550 Bonvoy points for every 1,000 you send, a 55% premium that runs through 11:59 p.m. ET on June 30. That's an unusually aggressive number for a hotel transfer — most Chase-to-Marriott promos top out around 25–30%.

The catch is the one that always applies to hotel points: Bonvoy is a soft currency, so the math only works if you have a concrete redemption that prices out well, ideally a category 6–8 property or a five-night award where the fifth night is free. Speculative transfers into Marriott rarely pay off, even at 55%. If you've been eyeing a specific summer or fall stay, though, this is the window to top up your balance.
