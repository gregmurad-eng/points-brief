Lead Story

Chase pushes 55% to Marriott — the richest transfer bonus on the board

Through June 30, Chase Ultimate Rewards is transferring to Marriott Bonvoy at a 1:1.55 rate — turn 1,000 points into 1,550 Bonvoy. It is the single largest live bonus across any of your programs this month, and it stacks naturally with Bonvoy's fifth-night-free on award stays.

The caveat is the math. Chase points are worth roughly twice a Bonvoy point, so a 55% bonus does not fully bridge the gap on paper. This only pencils out if you have a specific high-category redemption in mind — a Category 7–8 aspirational property or a sweet spot inside the Bonvoy chart — rather than a speculative transfer. Move points to the booking, not to the account. Details at Frequent Miler and AwardWallet.

Today's Move

Don't chase the Southwest clock

The June 5 Southwest deadline is the loudest offer this week, but loud isn't the same as valuable — Southwest points run about 1.3 cents against Chase's 2. Only transfer if a specific Wanna Get Away fare is already in your cart. The disciplined play this month is the 55% Marriott bonus, and even that only when a real high-category stay is booked. If nothing's on the calendar, the best move is no move: hold your balances.
