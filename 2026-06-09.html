Today's Move

◆ The Signal

Don't reflexively chase the 55% Marriott bonus. Open the Marriott award you actually want first, note the exact points needed, then transfer only that amount from Chase (plus a small buffer). Transfers are one-way and instant — moving "just in case" locks flexible points into a weaker currency. If you have no Marriott booking in sight, the better move today is using your Events by Amex access before it disappears tomorrow.
