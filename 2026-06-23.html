Lead Story

One Week Left: The June 30 Transfer-Bonus Cliff

Three of the strongest transfer promotions of the quarter all expire the same day, and that day is now seven mornings away. The headliner is Chase Ultimate Rewards to Marriott Bonvoy at a 55% bonus — 1,000 Chase points become 1,550 Bonvoy points through June 30. That rate started the spring at 65%, stepped down to 55% on May 16, and disappears entirely at month's end, so this is the floor before it hits zero.

Alongside it, American Express Membership Rewards is running 25% to Flying Blue and 20% to Marriott Bonvoy, both also closing June 30. For anyone sitting on a near-term Marriott redemption, the Chase route is the better feed right now; for SkyTeam premium-cabin hunters, the Flying Blue lift is the one to watch. The usual discipline applies harder than ever this week: transfers are one-way and irreversible, so confirm award space is bookable before you move a single point. Read the current landscape at Frequent Miler and The Points Guy.
